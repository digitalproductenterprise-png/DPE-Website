Refund Policy

Last updated: May 2026

We stand behind the quality of everything we sell. This policy sets out your rights and our commitments regarding refunds, in accordance with the Consumer Rights Act 2015 and the Consumer Contracts Regulations 2013.

1. Digital Products

Your statutory rights

Under the Consumer Contracts Regulations 2013, you have a 14-day right to cancel a digital product purchase — unless you have expressly consented to immediate delivery and acknowledged that your right to cancel is lost once the product is downloaded or accessed.

At the point of purchase, we ask customers to confirm that they consent to immediate delivery and understand this means they lose their statutory 14-day cancellation right. If you did not see or confirm this at checkout, your right to a 14-day cancellation may still apply — please contact us and we will assess your request.

Our goodwill refund policy

Beyond statutory rights, we operate the following goodwill policy for digital products:

  • Defective product — if a digital product is technically faulty, corrupted, or significantly different from its description, we will offer a replacement or full refund
  • Duplicate purchase — if you accidentally purchase the same product twice, we will refund the duplicate payment
  • Unable to access — if you cannot access or download your product and we are unable to resolve the issue within 48 hours, we will issue a full refund

We do not offer refunds on digital products simply because you changed your mind, found the material was not what you expected based on the description, or decided not to implement the strategies. Please review product descriptions carefully before purchase.

2. AI Agency Services

AI Audit

The AI Audit is a one-time service delivered during and after a discovery session. Once the audit has been delivered (in writing or verbally), it is considered complete and non-refundable. If you cancel before the audit session takes place, we will refund any fees paid minus any reasonable preparation costs already incurred.

Fixed-Scope Projects

For fixed-scope implementation projects, the following applies:

  • Cancellation before work commences: full refund of any advance payment
  • Cancellation after work has begun: fees for work completed to date are payable and non-refundable; any remaining advance payment will be returned
  • Completion of the project: all fees are non-refundable once the project has been delivered and accepted

Monthly Retainer

Monthly retainer fees are billed in advance and are non-refundable for work already carried out within that billing period. If you cancel mid-month, your retainer access continues until the end of the period paid for. No partial-month refunds are issued. Cancellation requires 30 days' written notice as set out in your service agreement.

3. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, please follow these steps:

1
Email us at support@digitalproductenterprise.com with the subject line "Refund Request"
2
Include your name, the product or service purchased, the date of purchase, your order reference (if applicable), and the reason for your refund request
3
We will respond within 3 working days to acknowledge your request and let you know the outcome
4
Approved refunds are processed within 5–10 working days back to your original payment method

4. Consumer Rights Act 2015

Nothing in this policy affects your statutory rights under the Consumer Rights Act 2015. Under this Act, services must be carried out with reasonable care and skill. If a service we provide does not meet this standard, you may be entitled to a repeat performance or a price reduction. We will always act in good faith to resolve any such situation.

5. Disputes

If you are unhappy with the outcome of a refund request, please let us know by emailing support@digitalproductenterprise.com. We will escalate your complaint internally and aim to provide a final response within 14 days.

If you remain dissatisfied, you may refer your dispute to an alternative dispute resolution (ADR) scheme or seek advice from Citizens Advice (citizensadvice.org.uk) or Trading Standards.

6. Contact

For any questions about this Refund Policy, please contact us at support@digitalproductenterprise.com.
